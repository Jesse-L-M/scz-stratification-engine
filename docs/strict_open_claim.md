# strict-open v0 claim

## Core claim

`strict-open v0` is a public cognitive stability and trial-noise audit engine for estimating stable baseline-like cognitive burden versus transient state noise from fully public schizophrenia-relevant data.

## What this build is trying to prove

This build is trying to prove that a fully public workflow can produce a technically credible, bounded estimate of stable baseline-like signal and visit-level state noise, with outputs that surface uncertainty and identify ambiguous or noisy visits rather than overclaiming precision.

Those outputs may include biology-context or evidence-card style interpretation layers and support cohort-integrity review, but `strict-open v0` does not claim sponsor-grade enrichment, a final commercial-grade decision product, or discovery of novel biological subtypes.

## Explicit non-claims

- No sponsor-grade enrichment.
- No treatment recommendation.
- No treatment-response engine.
- No persistent cognitive burden under real medication follow-up.
- No gated-data claims.
- No claims of discovering novel biological subtypes.
- The goal is to estimate stable baseline-like burden versus transient state noise, not to define new disease subtypes.
